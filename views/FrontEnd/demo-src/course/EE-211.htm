Course Description

D.C. and A.C. circuits, transformers, single and three-phase distribution systems, A.C. motors and generators.

Remarks

This course has proctored exams delivered through the College of Engineering, The proctors and the exams are handled outside of the World Campus Proctor Portal. For more information, contact Melissa Stark (mis3@psu.edu).
